Study And Work

Working Professionals opt for distance MBA Program to continue to Work while they pursue their MBA. They Enjoy the Flexibility of Distance Learning while Earning.

Distance MBA

Distance MBA costs less than a full-time, regular programme. This is a very economically viable option, easy on the pockets.

Convenience of Time

Students may study over the weekends or dedicate few hours of the day, while managing Professional lives.

Convenience of Place

You are the master of your studies. You get to decide how much time you want to dedicate to studies, at what pace, where and when.

Employers Acceptance

Employers of different sectors are more open to a distance learning degree. They encourage Employees to learn because it adds to their set of skills and expertise.

UGC / DEC Approved

Distance learning programmes must be accredited by the Distance Education Council (DEC) and approved by University Grants Commission (UGC) of India.

Contact Us

Step 1

Please leave us your name, phone, email and message. Our educational councellor will get back to you to understand your profile and assess the best course for you for your career advancement. This would be completely free of cost.

Councelling

Step 2

The councelling will happen over a phone call, where we would ask you many things about your career prefrences. Based on your answers we will analyse the best suitable course for you and also try to suite your budget.

Course Selection

Step 3

We will find various different course options for you and again discuss with you how and when you would be comfortable to start and also explain the entire course contents so that you can make the right decision.

Course Admission

Step 4

Once you decide to pursue a certain course, we guide you through the entire admission process and assist you till you start the course and settle down. Your progress is our priority. Your Success is our Goal.
